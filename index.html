What is Adire?

Adire is a traditional indigo-dyed cloth made in southwestern Nigeria by Yoruba women, using infinite variety of resist-dyeing techniques. It is more than just a fabric; it is a canvas of culture, storytelling, and artistic expression passed down through generations. At Eury Textiles, we preserve this ancient craft while infusing it with modern elegance for the contemporary soul.
